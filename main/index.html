About the Elections

The University Student Government Elections are held annually to elect representatives who will advocate for student interests and contribute to the development of our university community. The elections provide an opportunity for students to engage in the democratic process and have their voices heard.

How to Participate

Step 1: Register

Create your account to participate in the elections.

Step 2: Vote

Cast your vote for your preferred candidates.

Step 3: Results

View the election results after voting concludes.

Important Notices

  • Ensure your account is verified before the election date.
  • Voting will be open from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM on May 16.
  • Results will be announced on May 17.

Security Notice

Your vote is confidential and secure. We use advanced encryption to protect your information.
